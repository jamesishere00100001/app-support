Privacy Policy

Summary: Chainlog does not collect, store remotely, or transmit any personal data. All your ride data stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how Chainlog ("the App"), developed by James Attersley ("we", "us", or "our"), handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Last updated: 2 April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Chainlog has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no network connectivity for data transmission.

All data you enter — including ride distances, durations, dates, and notes — is stored locally on your device only. This data is never sent to us or to any third party.

2. How We Use Your Data

Because we do not collect any data, we have no data to process, store, or share. Your ride history is used solely by the App on your device to display your logs to you.

3. Third Parties

Chainlog does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We have no access to this data. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Data Retention

No personal data is retained by us. Any data stored by the App is held solely on your device. You can delete individual ride entries within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have rights including the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your personal data. Since we do not collect or process any personal data, these rights are not directly applicable in the context of this App.

If you have any questions about your rights or this policy, please contact us using the details below.

6. Children's Privacy

Chainlog does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. As no data is collected by the App, it is safe for use by all age groups in this respect.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
