About Chainlog

Chainlog is a cycling log app for tracking your rides. Record distances, durations, routes, and notes — all stored privately on your device. This page covers common questions and how to reach us for help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a new ride?
Tap the + button on the main screen to add a new ride. You can enter the distance, duration, date, and any notes you'd like to save.
Can I edit or delete a ride I've already logged?
Yes. Tap on any ride in your history to view it, then use the edit or delete options to make changes.
Will my ride data be backed up?
Chainlog stores all data locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPhone, your app data will be included in your device backup automatically. We recommend keeping iCloud Backup turned on to protect your ride history.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
Deleting Chainlog will remove all locally stored ride data from your device. Make sure you have a backup before uninstalling if you want to preserve your history.
The app crashed or isn't behaving correctly. What should I do?
Try closing the app fully and reopening it. Check that your device is running a supported iOS version and that Chainlog is updated to the latest version from the App Store. If the issue continues, please get in touch.
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
We're always looking to improve Chainlog. Send us an email — describe the issue or idea in as much detail as you can.
