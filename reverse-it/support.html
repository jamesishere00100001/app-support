About Reverse-It

Reverse-It is a fun party game for groups. Take turns, follow the prompts, and see who comes out on top. This page covers common questions and how to get in touch if something goes wrong.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a game?
Open the app and tap New Game. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your group and begin play.
How many players can join?
Reverse-It is designed for groups of 2 or more players. Everyone plays on the same device — no accounts or additional devices needed.
Can I customise the game settings?
Yes. Tap the settings icon before starting a game to adjust options such as round length and difficulty.
The app crashed or isn't responding. What should I do?
First, try closing the app fully and reopening it. If the problem persists, ensure your device is running a supported version of iOS and that the app is up to date in the App Store. If it continues, please contact us using the email below.
How do I report a bug or give feedback?
We'd love to hear from you. Send us an email and describe what happened — screenshots are always helpful.
Is there an Android version?
Reverse-It is currently available on iOS only. We have no current plans for an Android release.
