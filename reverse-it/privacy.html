Reverse-It

Privacy Policy

Summary: Reverse-It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how Reverse-It ("the App"), developed by James Attersley ("we", "us", or "our"), handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Last updated: 2 April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Reverse-It has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no network connectivity for data transmission.

All game data — including player names, scores, and settings — is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to us or any third party.

2. How We Use Your Data

Because we do not collect any data, we have no data to process, store, or share.

3. Third Parties

Reverse-It does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We have no access to this data. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Data Retention

No personal data is retained by us. Any data stored by the App (such as game history or settings) is held solely on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have rights including the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your personal data. Since we do not collect or process any personal data, these rights are not directly applicable in the context of this App.

If you have any questions about your rights or this policy, please contact us using the details below.

6. Children's Privacy

Reverse-It does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. As no data is collected by the App, it is safe for use by all age groups in this respect.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
